Upcoming Events


Annual Lancer Swim Team Fundraiser

The Lancer Swim Team's annual fundraiser will begin September 1st and continue through September 14th. Swimmers will be going door-to-door in surrounding neighborhoods selling coupon booklets. Each booklet contains over 200 coupons, valued at $100. Each coupon booklet costs $15.00.

Dive-In Movie

Join us on November 15th at 7:00pm for a dive-in movie at the Lancer Aquatic Center. Tickets will be sold at practices starting November 1st through November 14th. Tickets are priced at $5.00 a piece. Family and friends welcome!

Swim Team Banquet

Help us wrap up another amazing season at our end of the year banquet on December 12th at 6:30pm. The event will take place in room 222 at the Lancer Aquatic Center. Dinner and dessert will be catered by Chick-fil-a. All friends and family welcome!
